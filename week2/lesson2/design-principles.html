White Space and Clean Design

Apple

www.apple.com

Apple's website is an excellent example of the principles of white space and clean design. The sections have a maximum of 6 words per product, text in which we can see that it is hierarchical, this gives the user a lot of white space to breathe on the page, the product image is the main attraction. It is what will lead the user to fall in love with it and buy it.

repetition

Mercado Libre

www.mercadolibre.com

I consider Mercado Libre to be a great example of Repetition. On the web page we can see that in addition to everything being well aligned, both the text and the images. There are many induced repetitions such as the green letters where the greatest benefits of buying a product are seen, we also see that the products that are on sale have a blue sign that says "deal of the day". This allows the user to navigate more efficiently on the website. Since if I only look for products on sale I only look at the blue signs and if I have already seen a product the next thing I look for are the benefits that are in green.

Alignment

Amazon

www.amazon.com

At Amazon we see clear examples of alignment. All products are aligned with each other in a grid. The texts are all aligned to the right and also with a hierarchy that allows identifying the information from the most important to the least important. All this makes it very easy to navigate the website and enjoy browsing.
