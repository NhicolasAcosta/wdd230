About Us

Our company was born from the experience we have had in our city of Carlsbad, a very warm coastal city. And what do we usually do when we go to the beach to cool off a bit? Yes, a drink to accompany this weather is perfect.

Bountiful Foods is a company specializing in locally grown organic foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, however they have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Our company sells fruits and vegetables directly from farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers. The owner-operated business caters to traffic at its store in Carlsbad and, of course, its food trucks. We deliver and fulfill those orders over the phone and through the website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including freshly prepared, organic fruit drinks and fruit bags, to local people and tourists visiting coastal areas.
