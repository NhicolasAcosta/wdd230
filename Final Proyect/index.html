Our City

Carlsbad is known for its picturesque character and its hot springs, discovered in the late 1800s. It is an ideal place to relax and unwind in its spas, but the truth is that you can do many more things. The original source of hot springs you can visit; We recommend that you do not lose detail of the town, it will surprise you!

Due to its location, low population density and the high educational level of its school district, the city is known for its high quality of life. Therefore, houses are usually more expensive compared to other cities. In Forbes magazine's annual ranking of the "most expensive ZIP codes" in the US, the city of Carlsbad was listed as one of the most expensive ZIP codes. In a recent article published by "San Diego's Riviera" magazine, the magazine stated that the most expensive homes in San Diego County were found in "Rancho Santa Fe" averaging more than $2 million per home. Median home value in La Jolla is $1.4 million and Carlsbad is just over $1 million.
